Description of the 4th visualization variation

The 4th variation focuses on showing the total number of graduates for their respective Majors and their appropiate characteristics such as
how many of these people are employed, unemployed, employed full time.
There were some majors in the data that had a very high number of people. So this graph majorly focuses on the majors that have a
total less than 1 000 000. Those major that do reach this point are simply capped at that point.
