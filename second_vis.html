Description of second visualization variation

The second variation focuses on showing the majors who make the most money in terms of their median salaries
These majors with the highest median salaries are placed near the top. While the majors who make the least money in terms of their median salaries are at the bottom of the graph. In other words, these medium salaries were sorted from descending order.
