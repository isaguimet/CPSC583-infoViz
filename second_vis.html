Description of second visualization variation

Design Concept: To show which are the majors that are making the most money in terms on their median salaries

I wanted to show these in terms of their median salary because the majority of the people make money within this category.
Thus, majors with the highest median salaries are placed near the top. While the majors who make the least money
in terms of their median salaries are at the bottom of the graph. In other words, these medium salaries were sorted from descending order.
