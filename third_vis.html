Description of the 3rd visualization variation

The 3rd variation focuses on showing the total number of graduates for their respective Majors and other information such as
how many of these people are employed, unemployed and employed full time.
Note: There were some majors in the data that had a very high total number of graduates. So this graph majorly focuses on the majors
that have a total less than 1 000 000. Those major that do reach more than or equal to 1 000 000 are simply capped at that point.
