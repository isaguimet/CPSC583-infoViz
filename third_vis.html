Description of the 3rd visualization variation

Design Concept: To show how popular majors (which have the highest total numbers) compare with the least popular majors in terms of number of people employed vs number of people unemployed.
The 3rd variation focuses on showing the total number of graduates for their respective majors and other information such as
how many of these people are employed, unemployed and employed full time.

Note: There is 1 major (Business management and administration) which has a very high total number of graduates so in order too keep the scale meaningful I decided to cap this major off at 2 400 000 graduates.
